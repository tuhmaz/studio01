# **App Name**: Hausmeister Pro

## Core Features:

- User Roles & Secure Access: Robust authentication system supporting Admin, Team Leader, and Worker roles with specific permissions, allowing each user type to access relevant application features securely.
- Dynamic Job Site Management: Admins can define company headquarters and register new client job sites. The system will integrate with mapping APIs (like Google Maps) to automatically calculate travel times between HQ and job sites, adding compensated travel duration as needed.
- Shift & Task Time Tracking: Workers can clock in and out, specifying their current job site, with the app automatically calculating total working hours. This feature enforces GPS-based clock-in/out to ensure workers are at the correct location.
- Monthly Work Plan & Scheduler: Team Leaders and Admins can create and assign monthly work schedules to workers. The plan can be viewed in calendar, list, or map formats, displaying job details, assigned tasks, and timings for each worker.
- Task Evidence & Communication: Workers can upload 'before' and 'after' photos of their work directly from the job site and record audio notes to provide comprehensive task documentation.
- Comprehensive Reporting & Insights: Generate detailed monthly reports for accounting purposes, including total work hours, travel times, and task completion for each worker. An AI-powered tool will offer insightful summaries or flag unusual activity within reports, aiding management review.
- Real-time Team Overview: Team Leaders and Admins can view the real-time work status and GPS-verified locations of their team members, providing a quick overview of ongoing operations and worker deployment.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A professional and trustworthy deep blue (#2952A3) that reflects order and efficiency, ensuring good contrast against light backgrounds.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, cool blue-gray (#F0F2F4) to maintain a clean and airy interface, complementing the primary color without being distracting.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear sky blue (#7DC5E8) chosen for highlights, interactive elements, and critical information to guide user attention and convey clarity.
- Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for all text. Its modern, objective, and highly legible design ensures clarity and readability across both headlines and detailed task information, aligning with the app's professional and functional nature.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, line-based icons for clarity. Icons should visually represent tasks, locations, time tracking, and user roles effectively, ensuring immediate recognition of functions.
- A structured and organized layout focusing on clean data presentation, with adaptable dashboards for different user roles. The design will prioritize clear hierarchy and responsiveness across various device sizes to optimize usability in the field and office.
- Subtle and functional animations to provide instant feedback on user actions, indicate data loading, and facilitate smooth transitions between views. These animations should enhance user experience without being intrusive or distracting.
